How it works

Four steps, every day

Step 1

Plan
Open the plan (or ask Helper 1) for today's video idea + caption.

Step 2

Film
Shoot the one line on your phone — no editing needed.

Step 3

Edit
Upload the clip to Helper 2. It adds captions and cleans it up.

Step 4

Post
Post the video with the caption to Reels, TikTok, Shorts, Facebook.
