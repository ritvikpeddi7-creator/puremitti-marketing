Your two AI helpers

Agent 2 · The Video Editor

Edits your videos for you

You film raw clips on your phone. This does the editing — cuts, captions, and effects — and gives you a finished video to post.

1

Film

Shoot your clips on your phone — talking to camera plus a few product shots. No editing.

2

Upload

Drop the clips into the video app (Submagic or Captions — links below).

3

Pick a style

Choose a caption style. It auto-trims, adds animated captions, and cleans it up.

4

Post

Download the finished video and post it to Reels, TikTok, Shorts, and Facebook.

Recommended
Submagic ↗

Upload a clip → auto captions, cuts, and effects. Built for short vertical videos. Free trial, then a small monthly cost.
Alternative
Captions ↗

Similar all-in-one editor with an "AI Edit" that cleans up a raw clip and adds captions. Phone app + web.
The full loop: Agent 1 gives you the idea + caption → you film it → Agent 2 edits it → you post. Use the Planner's caption as the on-screen hook in the video app.
