The strategy in one screen

We don't need more reach. We need to own an intersection.

The competitor research shows a clear white space. Every one of the next 30 videos is engineered to plant Pure Mitti in the middle of it — and to be filmed in a handful of batch sessions, not thirty separate shoots.

What the competition leaves open

AyurvedicRanavat, Fable & Mane, UMA — aspirational, but premium-priced, skincare/hair only, not handmade.
HandmadeOsmia, Chagrin Valley — real craft, but no Ayurvedic story and dated content.
Clean homeBlueland, Branch Basics — home-only, clinical tone, no heritage or face.
Pure Mitti is the only brand at the intersection of Ayurvedic heritage + genuinely handmade + body and home + an accessible price + a real founder with local NJ roots. Every post reinforces that intersection.
Who we're talking to

Three people, one shelf

The Clean-Swap Parent
30–45 · NJ / suburban

Cutting toxins for the family. Loves handmade, local, and "ingredients I can pronounce." Buys to protect people she loves.

The Ritual Seeker
25–35 · wellness-native

Deep into Ayurveda, self-care and the "clean girl" aesthetic. Buys the ritual and the story as much as the bar.

The Conscious Gifter
all ages · discovers on IG/Etsy

Wants a thoughtful, sustainable gift that doesn't feel cheap. Converts on gift sets and beautiful unboxings.

How it gets made

One shoot, four cuts. A month in three sessions.

Platform system

ReelsPrimary growth engine — optimize the hook and saves.
TikTokSame cut — lean into ASMR, trends, raw authenticity.
ShortsSame cut + a keyword-rich title for long-tail search.
FacebookFounder stories, reviews, community — longer captions + links.
Cadence1 short-form/day + stories & reposts. Consistent beats perfect.
TimingIG 11a–1p & 7–9p ET · TikTok 6–10a & 7–11p ET (test & adjust).

Batch production

The making benchAll ASMR / process + ingredient B-roll (every Tue + Mon).
Founder, natural lightEvery to-camera founder & education talking head (Wed + explainers).
Bathroom / shelf / lifestyleSwaps, routines, gifting, offers.

Bank a B-roll library every session — hands, pours, textures, close-ups — to reuse all month. Trend (Thu) & UGC (Fri) are filmed reactively, week-of.
