# Pure Mitti — 30-Day Social Content Playbook

*Non-toxic, Ayurveda-inspired personal & home care · Basking Ridge, NJ · puremitti.com*
*Platforms: Instagram Reels · TikTok · YouTube Shorts · Facebook — one shoot, four cuts.*

> This is the editable master. The visual/presentable version is the published playbook page. The competitor research is in `PureMitti_Competitors.xlsx`. Anything in **[confirm]** is a real detail to verify before posting — never publish an unverified claim.

---

## 1. Strategy in one screen

**Positioning:** Pure Mitti makes the clean, Ayurveda-rooted personal- and home-care rituals a former ad executive built for her own family — handmade in small batches in Basking Ridge, NJ.

**The ownable gap (from competitor research):**
- *Ayurvedic beauty* (Ranavat, Fable & Mane, UMA) — aspirational but premium-priced, skincare/hair only, not handmade.
- *Natural soap makers* (Osmia, Chagrin Valley) — real craft, but no Ayurvedic story and dated content.
- *Clean home brands* (Blueland, Branch Basics) — home-only, clinical tone, no heritage or face.
- **Pure Mitti's white space →** the only brand combining Ayurvedic heritage + genuinely handmade + body *and* home + accessible price + a real founder with local NJ roots. **Every post reinforces this intersection.**

**Audience:** (1) The Clean-Swap Parent (30–45, NJ), (2) The Ritual Seeker (25–35, wellness-native), (3) The Conscious Gifter (all ages, IG/Etsy).

## 2. Weekly pillar rhythm
Mon **Education** · Tue **Product/ASMR** · Wed **Founder/BTS** · Thu **Trend** · Fri **Social Proof** · Sat **Sustainability/Swap** · Sun **Soft Offer**.
Mix: Education 22% · Founder 15% · Product 15% · Proof 15% · Swap 15% · Trend 10% · Offer 8%.

## 3. Platform system — one shoot, four cuts
- **Reels** — primary growth engine; optimize hook + saves.
- **TikTok** — same cut; ASMR, trends, raw authenticity.
- **Shorts** — same cut + keyword-rich title for search.
- **Facebook** — founder stories, reviews, community; longer captions + links.
- **Cadence:** 1 short-form/day + stories/reposts. **Best times (ET):** IG 11a–1p & 7–9p · TikTok 6–10a & 7–11p (test & adjust).

## 4. Batch production — film the month in ~3 sessions
- **Session A · Making bench:** all ASMR/process + ingredient B-roll (Tue + Mon).
- **Session B · Founder, natural light:** all to-camera founder & education (Wed + explainers).
- **Session C · Bathroom/shelf/lifestyle:** swaps, routines, gifting, offers.
- Bank a **B-roll library** each session (hands, pours, textures). Trend (Thu) + UGC (Fri) filmed reactively, week-of.

## 5. Measure weekly
Track **Reach · Saves · Shares · Watch-through · Follower growth · Link clicks**. Keep the top 20% of hooks; cut what flops.
